Our destinations

|Masai Mara

Masai Mara a place located on the south east of Nairobi a five hour drive from Nairobi town where one can experience wildebeest migration and maasai villages.
The towns we visit are:Leleshwa,Siana and Magadi.

| Coastal Regions

In the coastal regions one can access safaris,snorkeling,maasai villages and sandy beaches like the famous Nyali beach and the Diani beaches.
The towns we visit are:Lamu,Malindi,Watamu,Mombasa and Ukunda.

| Rift Valley region

In the rift valley region there are various regions where one can visit very many places such as the lake Nakuru which has flamingoes in the national park,the Hells gate park and the soda lakes such as Elementaita.
The towns we visit are:Naivasha,Eldoret,Gilgil and Kitale.

Samburu trails |

Samburu trails are conservation areas teaming with wildlife where one can access buffalo springs, the Samburu National Park,the Samburu Sopa lodge.
The towns we visit are:Lentile Hills,Umoja and Olerai.

The Tsavo |

Tsavo consists of the Tsavo west and the Tsavo east national parks.This is the home of very many animal species in kenya.It is also ranked as one of the largerst and best managed National Parks in the world.
The towns we visit are:Tsavo,Taveta,Mtito Andei and Gazi

Mt Kenya Region |

The Mt Kenya has a vast variety of diffrent tourist attractions for our clients.This is to the windward and leeward side of the mountain.It consist of Mountain climbing regions,The Mt Knya conservancy and also the well renowned Ol Pajeta Conservancy for its scarc White Rhino species.The towns we visit are:Nyeri,Nanyuki,Meru,Embu and Kerugoya
